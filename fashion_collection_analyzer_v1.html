Notes: color analysis is deterministic. Materials, silhouettes, themes, and garment counts are model-based estimates using browser-side image classification. Results are useful for collection research and direction-finding, but not perfect garment parsing.

How it works

1) You choose a local folder. 2) Each image is sampled for color data. 3) A browser-side vision model estimates garments, materials, silhouettes, and thematic cues. 4) The app aggregates all looks into one collection report.

Best results come from runway, lookbook, or editorial images with one clear look per frame.

How it works

1) You choose a local folder. 2) The app crops toward the centered runway look. 3) Color extraction uses that outfit-focused region instead of the whole frame. 4) Browser-side classification makes look-level decisions for garments, materials, silhouettes, and themes, then aggregates the collection.

Best results come from runway, lookbook, or editorial images with one clear look per frame.

1. Color palette

2. Materials used

Estimated materials/textures aggregated across all looks. Values are signal strength per look, not raw classifier decimals.

3. Silhouettes / theme

Silhouette and theme are counted by look. A look is either tagged or not tagged, so the output is shown as look counts and percentages.

4. Garment types and counts

Estimated garment frequency across the collection, using primary-garment and outer-layer decisions to reduce false counts such as trench coats.
